Jason Downing

Jason Downing is an undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Massachusetts, Lowell. Jason's hobbies include mountain biking, skiing, hiking, and coding.

He currently works for the Engaging Computer Group at UMass Lowell, and recently finished an internship at Fidelity Investments as a technical intern during Summer 2015. Jason will also be working at Fidelity Investments again next summer as an intern working on Android development.

Jason is active on Github and has various projects for school, as well as side projects with friends and on his own.
